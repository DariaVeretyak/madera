Privacy Policy

At Madera Consulting (“Madera”), your privacy is of paramount importance. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you engage with our business consulting services. By using our website, products, or services, you agree to the collection and use of your personal information as outlined in this policy.


1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect personal data that you voluntarily provide when interacting with Madera, including but not limited to:


Name

Email address

Phone number

Company name

Job title

Payment details

Any information you provide during consultations or correspondence

1.2 Automatically Collected Data

When you visit our website, certain information may be automatically collected:


IP address

Browser type

Device type

Browsing history and interaction with our website

Cookies and usage data (more on Cookies in Section 6)


2. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information collected to provide and improve our services. The purposes include:


Providing business consulting services

Communicating with you regarding your inquiries, bookings, or projects

Personalizing the content and experience on our website

Processing payments and issuing invoices

Sending updates, marketing materials, and event information (with your consent)

Analyzing website traffic and user behavior to enhance our services


3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:


Service Providers: We may share your data with trusted third-party service providers (e.g., payment processors, email marketing platforms) to assist us in delivering our services.

Legal Obligations: If required by law, we may disclose your personal data in response to governmental requests or legal processes.

Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to the same privacy commitments.


4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. Once the information is no longer needed, it will be securely deleted or anonymized.


5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:


Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Correction: If the information we hold is inaccurate, you have the right to request corrections.

Deletion: You may request the deletion of your personal information, subject to any legal retention obligations.

Objection: You can object to certain uses of your data, such as receiving marketing communications.


6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. We use cookies to:


Remember your preferences

Analyze website traffic and performance

Enable certain functionalities

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings, but disabling cookies may affect your experience on our site.


7. Security of Your Information

Madera takes reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no online data transmission is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.


8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies when visiting them.


9. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The latest version will be posted on our website with the effective date.


By engaging with Madera Consulting, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from using our services.
